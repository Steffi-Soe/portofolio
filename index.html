OCR — KTP / SIM Identity Extraction
Problem

Manually transcribing customer identity data from Indonesian national ID cards (KTP) and driver's licences (SIM) was slow, inconsistent, and a recurring source of data entry errors.

Solution

A computer vision pipeline that detects, deskews, and segments ID card images, then extracts structured fields (NIK, name, address, DOB, expiry) via OCR with post-processing validation logic.

PythonOpenCVPaddleOCRregexNumPy
Automated structured extraction from physical identity documents — eliminating manual transcription.

OCR — Fuel Receipt Processing
Problem

Fuel expenditure records (struk pengisian BBM) existed only as physical receipts. Aggregating, auditing, and reporting on them required manual re-entry — time-consuming and error-prone at scale.

Solution

An OCR and parsing pipeline that captures receipt images, extracts transaction fields (date, volume, unit price, total, SPBU code), and outputs clean structured records ready for downstream reporting and analysis.

PythonOpenCVPaddleOCRpandasregex
Paper receipts converted to structured data — enabling automated fuel expense tracking and operational reporting.
📦
ROP Inventory Forecasting System
Problem

Reorder decisions were made reactively and based on intuition rather than data — leading to stockouts, excess inventory, and unpredictable procurement cycles across a large SKU base.

Solution

A multi-model forecasting engine with per-SKU model selection (XGBoost, SARIMA, Prophet, Exponential Smoothing, Croston) and MLflow tracking. Outputs include confidence signals and REVIEW flags — designed as a decision-support tool rather than a black box.

PythonXGBoostProphetstatsmodelsMLflowpandas
Data-driven reorder points with explicit uncertainty signals — transparent, auditable inventory decisions at SKU level.
